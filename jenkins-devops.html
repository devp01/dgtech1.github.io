Jenkins: CI/CD Automation for DevOps

Jenkins is a widely used DevOps automation tool for implementing Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD). It automates builds, tests, and deployments, improving software quality and delivery speed.

Jenkins integrates with source control, testing tools, containers, and cloud platforms through its extensive plugin ecosystem.

Despite newer CI/CD tools, Jenkins remains a critical DevOps skill due to its flexibility, maturity, and enterprise adoption.
